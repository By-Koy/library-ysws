Welcome to the library.

Write guides, build projects, earn free prizes:

What is this?

Library is a potential hackclub YSWS centered arround learning and giving information.

This is how it works:

A project can be anything technical, but it all starts with the guide. You may create your own or use someone elses, just make sure to make it yours!

Writing your book

Some projects won't have guides, and this is when you make your own!

A guide is a markdown document including steps to make a project and an explanation of its principles

Your guide will be graded on its storytelling, how well it explains its principles, and of course, creativity!

After your guide got rated, you will gain ink for the time you spent researching and the amount for every project using it.

*When making a guide you will also gain pages for the project you made

Ink is then used in the hobby shop to get you all sorts of fun, creative rewards!

Making projects

Now that you've found a guide, it's time to make it come alive

Make a project following the guide's steps and explanations. You will also have to document the process with devlogs

You will be graded on both understanding and creativity, so go nuts!

After your project gets rated, you will gain pages depending on the amount of hours you spent on it.

Pages can be used in the tech shop to get grants for anything that can help you make the next project.
